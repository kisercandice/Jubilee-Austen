Hi, I'm a developer that loves clean & elegant code.

A little about me.

My first career and first love is music. I studied the clarinet in college and graduate school and have made a living as a freelance musician, performing, recording, and teaching. In 2020, due to the Covid-19 pandemic, all of my performing and recording work stopped. This was the perfect opportunity to learn a new skill: coding!

Music is a language, and to communicate effectively, it requires an intense attention to detail. I have always been obsessed with getting things "just right", and I enjoy bringing that focus to building beautiful, functional, and user-friendly websites.

Hand-Coded HTML

My focus is writing clean, well-formatted semantic HTML5 by hand to make sure that the content is accessible, easy to read, easy to collaborate, and easy to trouble-shoot.

Well-Organized CSS

I pride myself on writing CSS that is easy to read and build on. I focus on keeping my CSS lean and fast to load, and I make it a habit to stay up to date on current best practices.

Ease Converting Designs into Code

You can trust me to take a designers PSD and quickly & accurately convert it into a webpage that is pixel-perfect match.
